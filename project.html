About CES

CES is a simple educational project created for students to understand how web apps work.



Purpose of the system


• The government receives many court cases.


• Officials & government lawyers need to understand each case clearly.


• A system is needed where all details of a case are shown in one place.


• With proper information, court cases can be closed faster.
